We specialize in two primary types of cleaning services:

1. Regular Cleaning

Our professionals will clean your house regularly, ensuring that it remains spotless every week. The regular cleaning services include:

  • Vacuum cleaning floors and walls
  • Decluttering and organizing the house
  • Disinfecting washrooms, sinks, toilets, bathtubs, mirrors, etc.
  • Deep cleaning of the kitchen, including surface cleaning, home appliance cleaning, cleaning dustbins, and removing trash

You can adjust the frequency of regular cleaning as per your needs. For example, you may opt for twice or thrice a week.

2. Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is a more extensive process and covers areas not included in regular cleaning. This service includes:

  • Cleaning of window frames, windows, door frames, and doors
  • Dusting of doors, baseboards, curtain rods, side tables, etc.
  • Cleaning of interior window panes
  • Cleaning areas behind appliances like refrigerators, microwave ovens, or washing machines
  • Deep cleaning of the kitchen, including kitchen tiles, cabinets, chimneys, kitchen jars, utensils, etc.
  • Deep cleaning of bathrooms, including shower heads, taps, tiles, etc.
